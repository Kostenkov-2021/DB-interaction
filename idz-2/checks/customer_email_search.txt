Запрос:
SELECT customer_id, any(customer_name) AS customer_name, customer_email,
       countDistinct(order_id) AS orders_count, sum(line_total) AS revenue
FROM idz2.orders_flat
WHERE positionCaseInsensitive(customer_email, 'customer120') > 0
GROUP BY customer_id, customer_email
ORDER BY customer_id;

Время выполнения: 0.038 с. Это сканирование, а не индексный OLTP-поиск.
